The World's First Gold-Backed Crypto Currency

What is INNcoin?

INNcoin (IndependenceCoin) is a new decentralized Gold-Backed Crypto-Currency that is used as a micro-gold weighted instrument in commerce.

100% Real Gold

INNcoin is backed by 100 grams of physical gold stored in one of Anthem’s Salt Lake City vaults. It’s designed to have a 12 month lifespan, with all 10 million coins to be mined by July 2015

Who's behind INNcoin?

The company behind INNCoin is Anthem Vault Inc, which is based in Las Vegas. All metal is vaulted in an independent professional world-class facility in Salt Lake City, UT.  Learn more

How can I acquire INNcoin?

Creating an account with Anthem Vault is currently the only way to obtain an MGC wallet to hold this new crypto-currency. It is free to create and there is no obligation to make a purchase.
